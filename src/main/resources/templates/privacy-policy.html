Privacy Policy

How ASWallet handles your personal data

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who we are

ASWallet is a digital wallet application for deposits, withdrawals, transfers, and transaction history. This policy explains what data we collect, why we use it, and what choices you have.

2. Data we collect

  • Account details: username, email, password (stored hashed), registration date.
  • Profile details: name, phone, avatar, account status.
  • Wallet data: balance, bank card last four digits, card holder name, expiry.
  • Transactions: amounts, types, categories, dates, sender/receiver usernames, status.
  • Settings: balance visibility and email notification preferences.
  • Messages exchanged with admin support through the in-app mailbox.
  • Security logs: login activity timestamps (admin audit may also review account events).

3. How we use your data

  • To operate your wallet and process deposits, withdrawals, and transfers.
  • To enforce daily withdraw limits and risk review rules on flagged transfers.
  • To send optional email notifications when you enable them in Settings.
  • To provide support through admin messaging and account recovery flows.
  • To keep the platform secure and prevent misuse of accounts.

4. Sharing and processors

We do not sell your personal data. Transfers may be assessed by the ASWallet risk microservice (sender/receiver usernames, amount, and risk-related metadata). Email delivery uses the configured mail provider. Database hosting runs on infrastructure you control in this demo deployment.

5. Retention

Data is kept while your account is active. You may clear transaction history from the app. Deleting your account removes your profile, wallet records, and related personal data from the main application database, subject to technical logs retained for security.

6. Your rights

  • Access and update profile details from Profile and Settings.
  • Export transactions as PDF from Data & Privacy.
  • Control email notifications and balance visibility in Settings.
  • Delete your account permanently from Account & Security.
  • Contact us for privacy questions using the Contact Us page.

7. Security

Passwords are hashed. Sessions are protected by Spring Security and CSRF checks on sensitive actions. No system is perfectly secure—use a strong unique password and keep your login private.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: Contact Us or email aswallet.noreply@abv.bg.
